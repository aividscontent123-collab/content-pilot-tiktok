Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Content Pilot is a private content creation and publishing workflow used by the account owner. The tool only uses connected social media access to authenticate the account owner, prepare approved videos for upload, publish approved videos, and check publishing status.

Data we access

  • Basic account identity needed to confirm which social account is connected.
  • Profile information and basic statistics when explicitly authorized.
  • Video upload or publishing status for content approved by the account owner.

Data usage

We do not sell personal data. We do not use connected account access to publish content without approval from the account owner. Access is used only for authentication, review, upload, publishing, and status checks.

Data retention

Content metadata may be kept in an internal dashboard for production tracking. Access tokens are managed through the connected OAuth provider and can be revoked by the account owner at any time.
